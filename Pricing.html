Free Trial

Still unsure? Try us risk-free! We offer a Free Trial to experience our support first-hand. A free trial of 4 hours is available within the first 7 days of signing up. Billing for your selected monthly plan will begin once the 4 hours are used or after 7 days—whichever comes first.
*Free Trial is not applicable on "Pay As You Go" and "On Demand" plans.

Rollover Policy

  • The rollover benefit is available on the 40, 80, and 160 Hours Virtual Assistant Plans.
  • Up to 25% of unused hours can be rolled over to the next billing cycle.
  • Rolled-over hours are valid for one cycle only—unused hours after that will expire.
  • Rollover hours are non-transferable, have no cash or credit value, and are forfeited if the subscription is canceled.

Bonus Rollover (Up to 50%)

  • Share The Task Weave’s latest blog post on LinkedIn, Facebook, or Instagram.
  • Send a screenshot or link of your post to your dedicated assistant.
  • You’ll get an upgraded rollover limit of 50% for that billing cycle.

For PAYG Clients

  • Rollover does not apply.
  • Billed monthly based on total hours used.
  • A $1/month account maintenance fee is applicable, regardless of usage.
